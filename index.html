About Big Oogman

Oogminguloid is a groundbreaking artist known for his loud noises and consistent drug references. With multiple platinum stickers on his laptop and a few hundred followers on instagram, he continues to push the boundaries of hip-hop and inspire a new generation of dickheads.

Born and raised on the streets of either China, Bali, or Baltimore depending on when you ask him, B00g the Dude discovered his passion for music at a young age. His unique style blends classic hip-hop with unnecessarily loud production, creating a sound that's both annoying and catchy.
